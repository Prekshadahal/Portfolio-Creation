Impact made by Internet and its technology in our daily lives.


Internet is defined as the networks of network. It was first created in the year 1969 as ARPANET by the United States Department of Defense. The first message to be distributed through the internet was "LO", which was an attempt at "LOGIN" by Charley S. Kline to log into the computer from University of California (UCLA), Los Angeles. Internet connects millions of computers together globally forming a network.


Now, talking about Internet Technologies it is a technical field that covers the necessary skills required to develop different applications on the Internet or Internet based systems, along with building other fields like E-commerce, cloud, mobile, and other web based technologies. Internet and its technologies has brought changes into various fields such as mobile application developments, access of wireless network and security, cloud computing which has brought various advantages in the lives of humans and many more. The positive impacts of the internet includes effective communication using emails and instant messaging feature that provides its service to any corner of the world. The technologies of internet also helps in business interactions and transaction. Beside this, Internet is also being used in various health sector while testing the reports of the patients and making records for different purposes. It is used by professionals in exchanging important data in a secretive manner. Education has also recieved a huge boost as an untouchable books and journals are available online from libraries across the world. One can easily browse the internet and do some resarch to gain knowledge about the topic of their interest.


As everything has its pros and cons there are certainly some negative impacts of Internet. Some of them are there are many websites which provide illegal or innapropriate materials online that isn`t user friendly and may not be suitable for a certain age group. Many teenagers are addicted to social networks which disrupts their life, mentally, physically and emotionally. Some people use internet with the purpose of hacking other people`s document and stealing them. At most cases internet has also been blamed to reduce the social relation as people get attatched to their devices and forget social interactions.


Thus, like every other thing Internet and its technologies has both its advantages and disadvantaes. It is our duty to properly use for our ownsake and also for other people around us.
